ü§î Before You Choose...

You just interviewed 5 segments. Before picking your 2 segments, think through:

  • Revenue potential: Which segments have the highest GMV?
  • Success rates: Which segments have fewer payment failures?
  • Competitive position: Where is competition weak?
  • Engineering complexity: Which segments need simpler features?
  • Long-term value: Which segments stick around (high LTV)?

üí° AI Mentor Recommendation

Focus on: E-commerce + SaaS

Why? These two segments have the highest GMV potential (‚Çπ12L + ‚Çπ8L = ‚Çπ20L monthly). E-commerce needs fast checkout which aligns with QuickPay's core strength in instant payments. SaaS requires recurring billing infrastructure which, once built, creates sticky long-term revenue.

üìö Framework: This applies Clayton Christensen's Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) - E-commerce is "hiring" payments to reduce cart abandonment (job: convert browsers to buyers), while SaaS is "hiring" payments for predictable recurring revenue (job: automate billing and reduce churn).

üè¢ Real Example: Stripe started by focusing on developers + online platforms (2010). They ignored restaurants and offline retail for 3 years, allowing them to build API-first infrastructure. By 2013, they had 70% market share in developer tools before expanding to broader markets.

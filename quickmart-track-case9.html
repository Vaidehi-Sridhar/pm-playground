üí° Bonus: Product Catalog Quality Challenge

40% of products have missing info or wrong photos. How would you improve catalog quality?

üí° Creative Solutions for Catalog Quality Improvement

1. Vendor Portal with Quality Scoring
Give vendors real-time quality scores (0-100) based on completeness, photo quality, and accuracy. Display leaderboard and offer better placement for high-quality catalogs. Gamify with badges for "Gold Standard Vendor."
2. AI Photo Matching System
Auto-detect mismatched product photos using computer vision. Flag products where image doesn't match category/description. Suggest stock photos from database or generate alerts for vendor review.
3. Crowdsourced Quality Checks
Let shoppers report missing info or wrong photos with one-tap buttons. Reward reporters with credits. Use reports to auto-prioritize catalog fixes and notify vendors.
4. Automated Data Enrichment
Integrate with product databases (barcode APIs, manufacturer feeds) to auto-fill missing info like weight, dimensions, ingredients. Especially valuable for packaged goods with universal product codes.
5. Quality Gates for Listing
Require minimum standards before products go live: at least 1 photo, basic fields filled, weight/price present. Show vendors a checklist: "3/5 required fields complete" with clear CTAs to finish.

Recommended Approach: Start with quality gates to stop low-quality listings, then add vendor portal with scoring for top 100 vendors (drive 70% of sales). Layer in AI photo matching to catch existing issues and crowdsourced reporting for long-tail catalog. Gamification drives behavioral change over time.

üí° AI Mentor Recommendation

Focus on: Busy Professional + Young Family

Why? Busy Professionals (‚Çπ12L GMV) value speed and reliability - the core promise of quick commerce. Young Families represent the highest GMV segment (‚Çπ18L) with predictable weekly bulk orders and loyalty potential.

üìö Framework: This applies Clayton Christensen's Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) - Busy Professionals are "hiring" QuickMart to save time during cooking emergencies (job: get groceries in 10 min, not 2 hours), while Young Families are "hiring" us for reliable weekly provisioning (job: never run out of baby essentials).
